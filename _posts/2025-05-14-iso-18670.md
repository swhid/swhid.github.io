---
title: SWHID standardized as ISO/IEC 18670
layout: post
---

On **April 23, 2025**, the Software Hash Identifier (SWHID) was adopted as an international standard: **ISO/IEC 18670:2025**.

This milestone ensures global recognition and reliability for SWHID, strengthening reproducibility, traceability, and cybersecurity.

- [ISO/IEC 18670 official page](https://www.iso.org/standard/89985.html)  
- [Software Heritage announcement](https://www.softwareheritage.org/2025/05/14/iso-standard-swhid/)
