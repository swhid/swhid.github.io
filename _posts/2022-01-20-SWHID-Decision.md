On **January 20, 2022**, during the annual Software Heritage meeting at UNESCO, the decision is taken to start working on creating an international standard capturing the essence of the Software Heritage Identifiers.

- [Software Heritage meeting at UNESCO](https://www.softwareheritage.org/2022/01/20/swh5years-page/)
